The scenic beauty of the temple grounds in all seasons, as well as the solemn atmosphere of the temple grounds, will undoubtedly touch the hearts of all tourists.

WHERE TO FIND

Kiyomizudera can be reached by bus number 100 or 206 from Kyoto Station. Visitors can take the Gojo-zaka or Kiyomizu-michi bus to the temple, which is a ten-minute uphill walk away. Alternatively, Kiyomizudera is about a 20-minute walk along the Keihan Railway Line from Kiyomizu-Gojo Station.

WHY VISIT

Kiyomizu-dera has been a UNESCO World Heritage Site since 1994. There are several other buildings on the grounds that have been designated as "national treasures," as well as waterfalls and well-known landmarks. Aside from that, the temple's wooden stage is unquestionably its most famous feature, providing an incredible panoramic view of Kyoto rising out of a sea of maple trees.
